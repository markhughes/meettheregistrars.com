What is this?

Hey! My name is Mark, I created this website to increase transparency of domain renewal prices. Although some give you a great registration price, most don't make it obvious what the long term cost will be.

So if you're here because your registrar has done this to you, take a look at this collection. It's updated when I'm aware of changes.
